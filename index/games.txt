=== Web Games ===

Nightwire — Hidden-role deduction where the clues are numbers: probe a seat to learn how many Ghosts sit in that window, and remember the Ghosts choose what they publish. [party, multiplayer] https://nightwire.benrichardson.dev

Gravity Golf — Slingshot your ball through a field of planets and bend it around gravity into the hole. [arcade, multiplayer] https://gravity-golf.benrichardson.dev

Snake Royale — Classic snake in a shared arena — eat, grow and cut your rivals off; last snake slithering wins. [arcade, multiplayer] https://snake-royale.benrichardson.dev

Rhythm Relay — Relay the beat down two lanes, tap in time to keep a shared combo alive, and take a lane each with a friend. [arcade, co-op] https://rhythm-relay.benrichardson.dev

Hexbloom — Pick a colour to bloom your blob across the honeycomb and claim more tiles than your rivals. [board, multiplayer] https://hexbloom.benrichardson.dev

Cipher Clash — Trace words on a shared letter grid and race to claim them before your rival does. [word, multiplayer] https://cipher-clash.benrichardson.dev
