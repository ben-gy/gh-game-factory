## Balance (the difficulty curve is the opponent)
Built the sim FIRST and let it referee. It caught two real problems invisible to unit tests and a short playtest:
1. **Solo dying too early** (~floor 3 for the bot) — eased the opening.
2. **4-player parties immortal** — running to the floor cap ~40% of the time, because hero upgrades compound multiplicatively and a purely polynomial monster ramp gets out-scaled forever. Fixed with a **geometric HP term past floor 5** (`RAMP_EXP`, pinned by a test) that guarantees termination, plus a depth/party-scaled on-screen swarm cap and depth-scaled contact damage.
Result (measured, deterministic): P(reach floor 2)≈1.0 (no floor-1 cliff), median floor solo 3 / duo 4 / four 7, deep tail closes (Delve 4-player p90 ≈ floor 11, timeouts ≤4%), revives engaged in co-op (0 solo / ~24 duo / ~67 four). Modes genuinely differ (Onslaught kills ≫ Gauntlet kills).
